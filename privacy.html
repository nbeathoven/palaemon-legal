Palaemon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Palaemon is a marine conditions advisory app for recreational boaters. This policy explains what Palaemon does and does not do with your data.

The short version

Palaemon does not collect your data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no servers of ours. The only network traffic is your device fetching marine data from public and licensed weather providers.

What Palaemon stores, and where

Your vessels, limits and thresholds, trips, passages, logbook entries, float plans, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device database (SwiftData). If you use iCloud, this data syncs through your private iCloud (CloudKit) database, which belongs to you — we have no server of our own and no technical ability to read it. Deleting the app (and its iCloud data via iOS Settings) removes it.

Location

With your permission, Palaemon uses your device's location while you are using the app to fetch marine conditions near you. Your coordinates are sent directly from your device to weather and marine data providers — NOAA / National Weather Service / CO-OPS, Tomorrow.io, and Visual Crossing — solely to retrieve forecasts, tides, currents, and observations for that area.

  • We do not receive, store, or log your location. It is never linked to your identity and never used for advertising or tracking.
  • Location access is optional. You can enter any coordinate by hand or pick a point on the map instead.
  • You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

Third-party data providers

Requests for conditions include only the coordinates being looked up and are subject to the providers' own policies: NOAA (U.S. government data), Tomorrow.io, and Visual Crossing. Palaemon contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking.

Sharing and export (you are in control)

Palaemon lets you share a float plan or trip summary with someone ashore when you choose to. These shares happen only when you initiate them, and the content goes wherever you send it via the iOS share sheet. Palaemon does not transmit anything on its own.

Notifications and background refresh

If you enable alerts, Palaemon refreshes saved trip conditions in the background and shows local notifications when forecasts pass your thresholds. This happens on-device; notification content never leaves your device.

Children

Palaemon is a general boating app and is not directed at children under 13.

Not a safety service

Palaemon is an advisory planning reference, not a navigation, safety-of-life, or emergency service. Always consult official sources and use your own judgment at sea.
