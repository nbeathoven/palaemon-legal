Palaemon

Marine conditions, your limits.

Palaemon is a marine conditions advisor for recreational boaters. Set the limits that matter for your boat — wind, waves, gusts, current, depth under your keel — and Palaemon compares real forecasts and observations against them, telling you plainly whether conditions are within, near, or over your limits.

What it does

  • Wind, gusts, waves, sea state, visibility and precipitation for your location, from NOAA and leading marine providers.
  • Station-based tide & current predictions — with honest availability when there's no station nearby.
  • Passage planning with leg-by-leg conditions, tide- and current-aware ETAs, and best departure windows.
  • Official NOAA ENC nautical charts, with offline chart packs.
  • Bar & inlet crossing advisories matched to your draft and the tide.
  • Alerts, float plans, widgets, Live Activities, and Apple Watch glances.
  • iCloud sync across your devices — no account, no login.

Honest by design

Palaemon never labels conditions "safe" or "unsafe." It shows sourced data, tells you where it came from and how fresh it is, and compares it to limits you chose. Where real data isn't available, it shows nothing rather than a guess. Palaemon is an advisory planning reference — not a navigation, safety-of-life, or emergency service. Always consult official sources and use your own judgment at sea.
